Welcome

Hi, I'm Celeste. I am a hobby photographer based on the Central Coast of California. From an early age I was drawn to the beauty of nature. Landscape and portrait photography have been my areas of passion. If I'm not out snapping photos I'm usually spending time with my two dogs or out cycling, all while seeking new places to capture beauty. Aside from my interest in photography I have a great interest in technology, and I am currently working on expanding my skills as a full-stack web developer. I am proud to say that I built this website! Hope you enjoy, thank you for stopping by.
